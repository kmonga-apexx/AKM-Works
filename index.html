AKM Works

AKM Works is a consulting company in the Northern Virginia area that provides consulting in various areas. Our primary two are Educational Tutoring and Furniture upcycling. Scroll to learn more!

Educational Tutoring

We provide 1-on-1, In-Home and Online tutoring in the local NOVA region in the following subjects: Math, English, Science, SAT/ACT and College Prep. We work with each student at their level and create individualized plans the set them up for success. Our goal is to help them implement learning skills in all aspects of their life to be effective in school and beyond.

Furniture

Shop for modern recycled furniture that fits your style and budget from our local marketplace. Everything from end tables, cabinets and desks! We upcycle furniture items and create designs that you find in high end furniture shops at a fraction of a price!
